What is Prof. Oak's Challenge?

The jist of the challenge is that you have to catch every single Pokemon available before obtaining the next badge. This means fully evolving your starter and catching & fully evolving every Pokemon you can before challenging the first gym leader, then catching and fully evolving every Pokemon available before the second gym, etc.

What is the point of this website?

The goal of this site is to provide a simple tool for people to track their progress in Prof. Oak Challenge runs without much overhead. Note that all data is stored locally, so your checklists will be saved between sessions but not across different devices.
