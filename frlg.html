Pre Badge 2

Since this section now contains Item evolutions, I've added an Item table.

Item How to Obtain Obtained?
Moon Stone #1
Mt. Moon - 1F, in the northwest area of the floor.
Moon Stone #2
Mt. Moon - B2F, southeast of the Super Nerd on a rock (hidden)
TM 46 (Thief)
Mt. Moon - B2F, on a plateau north of a Team Rocket Grunt in a closed-off room accessible from the ladder between the other two ladders on 1F. This will make acquiring the remaining 2 required Moon Stones much more efficient.
Moon Stone #3
Mt. Moon - Steal from wild Clefairy using Thief. Note that Clefairy has its highest encounter rate on B2F at 6%. Also, Clefairy has only a 5% chance of holding a Moon Stone. Good luck.
Moon Stone #4
Mt. Moon - Steal from wild Clefairy using Thief
Dome or Helix Fossil
Mt. Moon - Choose one after beating the Super Nerd
Rare Candy
Mt. Moon - 1F, near the southeast corner of the floor. Not required, but helpful for level grinding.
